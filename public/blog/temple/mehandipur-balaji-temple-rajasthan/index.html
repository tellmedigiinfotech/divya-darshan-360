Location- How to Reach/commuting- Flight/Train/Bus/Taxi Stand/Ferry/Port Air- whatever is available?

Mehandipur Balaji Temple is located in the Dausa district of Rajasthan, India. Here’s how you can reach the temple:

By Air: The nearest airport is Jaipur International Airport, which is about 113 kilometers away from the temple. From the airport, you can hire a taxi or take a bus to reach Mehandipur Balaji.

By Train: The nearest railway stations are Bandikui Junction, which is about 40 kilometers away, and Dausa station, around 60 kilometers from the temple. These stations are well-connected to major cities in India. From the station, taxis and local buses are available to reach Mehandipur Balaji.

By Bus: Regular bus services connect Mehandipur Balaji to nearby cities like Jaipur, Alwar, and Bharatpur as well as to other major cities in and around Rajasthan. The temple can be reached by state-run buses or private tour operators.

By Taxi: Taxis can be hired from any major city in Rajasthan to Mehandipur Balaji. This is a convenient option if you are traveling in a group or prefer a more comfortable journey.

There are no ferries or ports as the temple is inland and not near any significant water bodies.
