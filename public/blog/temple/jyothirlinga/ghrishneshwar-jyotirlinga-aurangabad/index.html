The Ghrishneshwar Jyotirlinga Temple is one of the twelve Jyotirlingas dedicated to Lord Shiva, making it a significant pilgrimage site for devotees. It is located near the famous Ellora Caves, adding historical and cultural importance to its spiritual significance.

    Annual Event at Ghushmeshwar Dwadashwa Jyotirling Mandir:

    Maha Shivratri

    Event: Maha Shivratri

    Date: Celebrated annually on the 14th night of the dark fortnight in the Hindu month of Phalguna, which typically falls in February or March.

    Significance: This festival is one of the most important events dedicated to Lord Shiva, attracting thousands of devotees who participate in night-long vigils, special poojas, and rituals.
