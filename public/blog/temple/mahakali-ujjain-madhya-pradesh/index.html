The Mahakali Temple in Ujjain is a revered shrine, primarily dedicated to Goddess Mahakali, also known as Harsiddhi Mata. It also houses idols of other deities such as Mahalakshmi, Mahasaraswati, Annapurna Mata, and Maheshwara, making it a significant spiritual destination.
