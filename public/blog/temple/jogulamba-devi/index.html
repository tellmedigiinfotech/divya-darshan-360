The Sri Jogulamba Bala Brahmeswara Swamy Devasthanam in Alampur is one of the 18 Maha Shakti Peethas, which are highly revered in Hindu mythology. The temple holds special significance as it is believed that the upper teeth of the goddess Sati fell here, making it a site of immense spiritual importance. Alampur is also known as Dakshina Kashi (Southern Kashi) due to its spiritual prominence.
