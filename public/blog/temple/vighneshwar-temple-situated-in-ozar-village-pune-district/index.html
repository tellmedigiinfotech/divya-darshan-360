i. Early morning activities start at 5:00 AM with the waking up of the deity, followed by Prakshalana Pooja at 5:30 AM.

iii. The major Maha Pooja takes place at 12:00 PM.

iv. Additional Prakshalana Pooja and Poshakh activities occur at 3:00 PM.

v. The evening concludes with Dhoopaarti and Aarti of the Avarana Deities at 8:00 PM, followed by Maha Aarti at 8:30 PM and Shej Aarti at 10:00 PM.

Provide Construction/ scientific information about the temple?

The Vighneshwar Temple in Ozar is renowned not just for its spiritual significance but also for its impressive architectural features. This temple, dedicated to Lord Ganesha, showcases a rich blend of mythological and cultural heritage, encapsulated within its construction and decorative elements.

Constructed by Chimaji Appa, the brother and military commander of Peshwa Baji Rao I, the temple underwent significant renovations, including the addition of a gold-covered shikhara (temple spire) after Chimaji Appa's victory over the Portuguese at Vasai Fort. These renovations are reflective of the typical Maratha architectural style which features elaborate gateways and a spacious courtyard.

The temple's design includes a grand entrance and is surrounded by a walled compound, which enhances its sanctity and grandeur. The central icon of Ganesha inside the temple is considered svayambhu (self-manifested), embodying a deep spiritual essence that attracts devotees from all over. The idol faces east and is flanked by brass images of his consorts, Siddhi and Riddhi, with detailed embellishments including sindoor, emeralds, and diamonds adding to its sacred aura.

Moreover, the temple's inner sanctum is adorned with colorful murals and sculptures that depict various religious narratives, contributing to the temple's spiritual and cultural narrative. This intricate work not only highlights the artistic heritage of the region but also serves as a medium for storytelling through religious art.

The temple also hosts major festivals like Ganesh Chaturthi and Ganesh Jayanti, which are celebrated with great pomp and show, illuminating the temple's importance in the cultural tapestry of Maharashtra.

For those interested in the detailed history and architectural specifics, visiting the temple offers a direct way to engage with the tangible aspects of this heritage. The serene environment of the temple, combined with its rich decorations, provides a profound spiritual and emotional experience for visitors.
