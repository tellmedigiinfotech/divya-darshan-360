Condensed information about the place

MAIN DEITY-

At the Shri Naina Devi Ji Temple, the main deity worshipped is Goddess Naina Devi, regarded as an incarnation of Goddess Sati, associated with the eyes. This temple is one of the 51 Shakti Peethas, where the eyes of Goddess Sati are believed to have fallen. Alongside the main deity, the temple also venerates other deities such as Lord Ganesha and Bhairava, who are considered important in the temple's spiritual tradition and are typically revered in conjunction with Goddess Naina Devi.
