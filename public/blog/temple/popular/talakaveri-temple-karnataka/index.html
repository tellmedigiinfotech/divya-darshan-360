Provide Construction/ scientific information about the temple

The construction of Talakaveri Temple incorporates traditional South Indian architectural elements, typically found in many Hindu temples in the region. These structures are often characterized by intricate stone carvings, towering gopurams (gateway towers), and detailed iconography representing various deities and mythological themes. The temple is designed to align with specific religious and astronomical beliefs, often incorporating Vastu Shastra principles, which dictate the spatial arrangement to maximize spiritual benefits.

Is there any special significance attached to the temple -like for eg. Kasar Devi in Uttar khand- it is said-

“ The region surrounding the Kasar Devi Temple has an enormous geomagnetic field or Paramagnetism, thanks to gaps in bands of radiation. As a result, Kasar Devi is endowed with a cosmic energy similar to that of Stonehenge in UK and Machu Picchu in Peru.”

Talakaveri Temple is considered especially significant because it marks the origin point of the River Kaveri, which is revered as a holy river in South India. The temple is situated at the Brahmagiri hill, which according to Hindu mythology, is where the river begins from a spring at a location identified by a tank. This site holds immense spiritual importance as devotees believe that the waters of the Kaveri have the power to cleanse sins and help attain salvation. The temple attracts pilgrims especially during Tula Sankramana, when the waters are said to gush forth at a predetermined moment.

Annual Event of the temple day and date?

The annual event at Talakaveri Temple is the Tula Sankramana, which typically occurs in mid-October. This significant festival marks the day when the holy water is believed to surge up from the spring at Talakaveri, the source of the River Kaveri. This event draws large numbers of devotees who come to witness and partake in the sacred waters, believed to have purifying properties. For specific dates and details for any year, it's advisable to check closer to the event, as astrological calculations can influence the exact date.
