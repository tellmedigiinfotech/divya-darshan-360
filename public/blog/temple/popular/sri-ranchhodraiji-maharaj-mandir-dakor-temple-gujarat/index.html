Construction and Scientific Information about Shri Ranchhodraiji Maharaj Mandir (Dakor Temple):

Construction
  • The temple was originally built in 1772 A.D. by Gopalrao Jagannath Tambwekar.
  • The architecture is a blend of Mughal and Maratha styles, with intricate carvings and a grand central dome.
  • The temple complex includes a large courtyard, multiple smaller shrines, and a sanctum sanctorum.
Scientific Aspects
  • The temple's design follows traditional Vaishnavite architectural principles, emphasizing symmetry and alignment with cardinal directions.
  • Construction materials include locally sourced stones and wood, chosen for their durability and aesthetic appeal.

Annual Event of the temple day and date?

The annual event at Shri Ranchhodraiji Maharaj Mandir (Dakor Temple) is Fagun Purnima, celebrated in the Hindu month of Phalguna (February-March). This festival is marked by grand celebrations and a large gathering of devotees who come to seek blessings from Lord Ranchhodraiji.

Monthly Event at Shri Ranchhodraiji Maharaj Mandir (Dakor Temple):
