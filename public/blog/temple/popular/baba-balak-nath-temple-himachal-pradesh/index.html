    Is this temple registered under the state govt/District administration? Provide if the information is visible on the site?

    Yes, the Baba Balak Nath Temple is registered under the state government of Himachal Pradesh and is managed by the district administration.

    Official Registration and Management

    • Government Oversight: The management and administration of the Shri Baba Balak Nath Temple Deothsidh were taken over by the Government of Himachal Pradesh on January 16, 1987, under the H.P. "Hindu Public Religious Institution and Charitable Endowments Act, 1984."
    • Temple Trust: The temple is managed by the Shri Baba Balak Nath Temple Trust Deothsidh, which is headed by the Temple Trust Commissioner-cum-Deputy Commissioner of Hamirpur, HP.
