Provide Construction/ scientific information about the temple?

Shree Mahalakshmi Temple, located in Kolhapur, Maharashtra, is an ancient temple dedicated to Goddess Mahalakshmi. The temple is a fine example of Hemadpanthi architecture, featuring intricate carvings and sculptures. The main sanctum houses the idol of Goddess Mahalakshmi, which is made of gemstone and weighs about 40 kilograms. The temple complex also includes several smaller shrines dedicated to various deities.
