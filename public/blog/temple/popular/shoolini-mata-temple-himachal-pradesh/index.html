Annual Event of the temple day and date?

The annual event at the Shoolini Mata Temple, known as the Shoolini Mela, is a significant festival celebrated with great enthusiasm. It is held over three days from June 21st to June 23rd. This festival, deeply rooted in local traditions, attracts large crowds with its vibrant celebrations including processions, cultural performances, and various community activities. It's a wonderful opportunity for visitors to experience the local culture and spirituality deeply connected with the temple​ (Himachal Story)​​ (HP Solan)​.
