Provide Construction/ scientific information about the temple?

The Mahabaleshwar Temple, dedicated to Lord Shiva, is an ancient structure known for its traditional Hemadpanthi architectural style, characterized by black stone construction and intricate carvings. The temple houses the "Mahalingam" of Lord Shiva, which is a significant religious and spiritual symbol.

Annual Event of the temple day and date?

The Mahabaleshwar Temple celebrates its annual event, known as Mahashivaratri, which typically falls in February or March. This festival is dedicated to Lord Shiva and involves special poojas, rituals, and cultural programs that attract numerous devotees.

Monthly Event of the temple - day and date?

The Mahabaleshwar Temple celebrates a monthly event on the day of "Pradosham," which occurs twice a month on the 13th day of each lunar fortnight in the Hindu calendar. Special poojas and rituals are performed during these days, focusing on the worship of Lord Shiva.
