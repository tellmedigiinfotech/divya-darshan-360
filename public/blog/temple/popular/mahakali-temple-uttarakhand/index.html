Provide Construction/ scientific information about the temple?

Construction Details
1. Architecture
  • The temple is situated on the highest mountain peak on the border between Pauri Garhwal and Almora districts.
  • The structure follows traditional North Indian temple architecture with a sanctum (garbha griha) and a spire (shikhara) adorned with intricate carvings.
2. Materials Used
  • Constructed using locally sourced stone and wood, which blend seamlessly with the surrounding natural environment.
  • Recent renovations have enhanced the structural integrity while preserving historical and cultural elements.
Scientific Significance
1. Location
  • Positioned at a high altitude, providing a panoramic view of the Himalayas, which adds to the spiritual and serene ambiance.
  • The geographical placement aligns with Vastu Shastra principles, believed to enhance the temple's spiritual energy.
2. Environmental Harmony
  • The construction integrates sustainable practices, using natural materials that ensure minimal environmental impact.
  • The temple’s design promotes natural ventilation and lighting, reducing the need for artificial resources.
