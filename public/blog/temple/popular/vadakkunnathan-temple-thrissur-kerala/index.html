Provide Construction/ scientific information about the temple ?

The Vadakkunnathan Temple, an architectural marvel, is a classic example of the Kerala style of architecture. Constructed in the traditional Kerala style, the temple features monumental towers, a Koothambalam (a theater hall), and exquisite murals depicting scenes from Mahabharata. The temple's main sanctum houses a lingam, representing Lord Shiva. The structure showcases the use of wood and stone, with intricate carvings and sculptures.

Annual Event of the temple day and date?

The annual event at the Vadakkunnathan Temple is the Thrissur Pooram, one of the most famous temple festivals in Kerala. It usually takes place in the Malayalam month of Medam (April-May), with the main day of Pooram falling on the Pooram star. This grand event features a spectacular display of caparisoned elephants, traditional music (Panchavadyam), and fireworks.
