Annual Event of the temple day and date?
The annual event of significant importance at Chaturshringi Temple is the celebration of the Navratri festival. This festival, dedicated to Goddess Durga, spans nine nights and is one of the most vibrant and widely celebrated events at the temple. The festival culminates on the tenth day, known as Dussehra or Vijayadashami, which usually falls in September or October, depending on the lunar calendar.
During Navratri, the temple is elaborately decorated, and various religious ceremonies and cultural performances are held. Devotees participate in large numbers, engaging in rituals and prayers. This period is marked by a special fair and a festive atmosphere, attracting thousands from Pune and beyond.
For the exact dates each year, since they vary according to the Hindu lunar calendar, it's recommended to check the temple's official website or contact the temple authorities closer to the festival period.
