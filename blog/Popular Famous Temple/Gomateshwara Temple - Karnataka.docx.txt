24. .Provide Construction/  scientific  information about the  temple?
Construction:
* Location: Shravanabelagola, Karnataka, India
* Main Attraction: 57-foot monolithic statue of Lord Gomateshwara (Bahubali)
* Material: The statue is carved from a single block of granite.
* Erected: Around 981 AD by Chamundaraya, a minister and commander of the Ganga Dynasty.
* Architectural Style: Dravidian architecture with Jain influences.
Scientific Aspects:
* Monolith: The statue is one of the largest free-standing statues in the world.
* Engineering: The precision of carving and stability of the statue demonstrates advanced engineering skills of the time.
