24. .Provide Construction/  scientific  information about the  temple?
Construction and Scientific Information about Kamakshi Amman Temple
Architectural Style:
* The Kamakshi Amman Temple is an exemplar of Dravidian architecture, characterized by its towering gopurams (gateway towers), intricately carved pillars, and expansive courtyards.
Main Features:
* Sanctum Sanctorum (Garbhagriha): The innermost sanctum houses the idol of Goddess Kamakshi.
* Mandapam: A hall for congregational worship and various rituals.
* Vimana: The temple tower above the sanctum, often adorned with intricate sculptures.
Materials Used:
* The temple is primarily constructed from granite and other locally sourced stones, ensuring durability and stability.
Scientific Aspects:
* The temple’s design incorporates ancient Vastu Shastra principles, aligning with cosmic and terrestrial energies to enhance spiritual experiences.
