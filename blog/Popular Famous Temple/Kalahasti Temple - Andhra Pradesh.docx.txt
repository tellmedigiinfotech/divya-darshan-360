24. .Provide Construction/  scientific  information about the  temple?
Construction and Scientific Information about Srikalahasti Temple:
* Architecture: The temple showcases Dravidian architecture with intricate carvings and a towering gopuram.
* Scientific Significance: The temple is renowned for its Vayu Linga, representing the element of wind among the Panchabhoota Sthalams.
* Historical Context: The temple has ancient roots, with contributions from various dynasties including the Cholas and Vijayanagara Empire.

29. Monthly Event of the temple - day and date?
Monthly Event at Srikalahasti Temple
The temple holds various poojas and rituals throughout the month, but a notable monthly event is the Pradosham. Pradosham is observed twice a month on the 13th day of each lunar fortnight, dedicated to Lord Shiva, and is considered an auspicious time for worship and seeking blessings.
