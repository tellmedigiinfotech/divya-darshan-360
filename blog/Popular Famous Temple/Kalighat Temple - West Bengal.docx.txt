Special Significance of Kalighat Kali Temple
Kalighat Kali Temple is one of the 51 Shakti Peethas, where the toes of the right foot of Sati are believed to have fallen. This gives the temple immense spiritual significance, drawing pilgrims and devotees from all over. The temple is renowned for its powerful deity, Goddess Kali, who is worshipped with deep reverence.
While it may not have geomagnetic properties like Kasar Devi, its historical and religious importance makes it a significant spiritual destination.
