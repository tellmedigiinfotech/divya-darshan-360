8.       Any specific Dress Code prescribed for entry to the place - men /women/for spl puja and festival days?
Dress Code for Entry to Mookambika Temple:
Mookambika Temple has a prescribed dress code for devotees to ensure decorum and respect within the sacred premises. Here are the details:
General Dress Code:
For Men:
* Allowed: Traditional attire like dhoti, mundu, or kurta-pajama. Shirts and t-shirts are generally acceptable.
* Not Allowed: Shorts, lungis, sleeveless tops, and caps.
For Women:
* Allowed: Traditional attire like sarees, salwar kameez, churidar, and half-sarees. Long skirts and tops that cover the shoulders and knees are also acceptable.
* Not Allowed: Short skirts, shorts, sleeveless tops, and tight or revealing clothing.
Dress Code for Special Poojas and Festival Days:
For Men:
* Recommended: Dhoti and angavastram (upper cloth) or kurta-pajama. It is recommended to avoid shirts and wear a traditional upper cloth during special poojas and festivals.
For Women:
* Recommended: Sarees or salwar kameez with dupatta. Traditional attire is highly encouraged during special poojas and festivals.
Additional Guidelines:
* Devotees should ensure that their attire is clean and modest.
* Footwear should be removed before entering the temple premises.
* Wearing a head covering (like a shawl or scarf) may be required for women in certain areas of the temple.

19. What is the procedure for reporting items lost in a temple?


Steps to Report Lost Items:
1. Visit the Temple Office:
* Location: The temple office is typically located near the main entrance or within the temple premises.
* Timings: Ensure you visit during the temple's operating hours (5:00 AM to 1:30 PM and 3:00 PM to 9:00 PM).
2. Inform the Security Personnel:
* Location: Security personnel are stationed at various points around the temple. Inform them immediately if you realize an item is lost.
* Details: Provide details about the lost item, including a description, where and when you last had it, and any identifiable marks.
3. Register the Lost Item:
* Lost and Found Register: The temple office maintains a lost and found register.
* Procedure:
   * Fill out the form with your personal details (name, contact information).
   * Provide a detailed description of the lost item (type, color, size, any unique identifiers).
   * Mention the approximate time and place where the item was lost.
4. Check for Found Items:
* Lost and Found Counter: Check if the item has already been found and handed over to the lost and found counter.
* Verification: You may need to verify your ownership by providing additional details or proof if necessary.
5. Follow Up:
* Contact Information: Provide your contact details so the temple authorities can reach you if the item is found later.
* Revisit or Call: Follow up by revisiting the temple office or calling them after a few days to check if your item has been recovered.
