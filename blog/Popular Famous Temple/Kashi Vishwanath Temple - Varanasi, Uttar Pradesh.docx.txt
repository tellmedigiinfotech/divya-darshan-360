A. MAIN DEITY:
* Lord Shiva (Vishwanath): The primary deity of the Kashi Vishwanath Temple is Lord Shiva, worshipped here as Vishwanath or Vishweshwara (Ruler of the Universe). This Jyotirlinga is one of the twelve holiest shrines of Lord Shiva.
B. OTHER DEITIES (Sub Deities):
* Maa Annapurna: Goddess of nourishment, an incarnation of Parvati, the consort of Lord Shiva.
* Kaal Bhairav: A fierce form of Lord Shiva, believed to be the guardian deity of Varanasi.
* Dhandapani: Another form of Lord Shiva known for his protective and benevolent nature.

The Kashi Vishwanath Temple, located in Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh, is managed by a trust established by the Government of Uttar Pradesh.
