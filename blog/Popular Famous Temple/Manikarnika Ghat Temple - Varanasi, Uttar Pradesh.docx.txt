Manikarnika Ghat Temple - Construction and Scientific Information
Construction:
* The Manikarnika Ghat Temple, part of one of the oldest and most sacred ghats in Varanasi, features traditional North Indian temple architecture.
* Constructed using stone and brick, the temple structures have intricate carvings and sculptures, characteristic of ancient Hindu temple design.
* The ghat itself is composed of broad stone steps leading down to the Ganges River, facilitating cremation rituals.
Scientific Information:
* The ghat is known for its continuous cremation activities, which are significant in Hindu beliefs about death and rebirth.
* The area’s geomagnetic field and its proximity to the Ganges River are believed to have a spiritually uplifting impact.
