Condensed Information about Ghrishneshwar Jyotirlinga Temple: 
A. Main Deity: 

Lord Shiva (Ghrishneshwar Jyotirlinga) 
B. Other Deities (Sub Deities): 

Goddess Parvati: Consort of Lord Shiva, symbolizing fertility, love, and devotion. 
Lord Ganesh: Son of Shiva and Parvati, the remover of obstacles. 
Lord Kartikeya: Son of Shiva and Parvati, the god of war. 
The Ghrishneshwar Jyotirlinga Temple is one of the twelve Jyotirlingas dedicated to Lord Shiva, making it a significant pilgrimage site for devotees. It is located near the famous Ellora Caves, adding historical and cultural importance to its spiritual significance.
