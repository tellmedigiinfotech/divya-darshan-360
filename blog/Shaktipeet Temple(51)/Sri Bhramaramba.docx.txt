29. Monthly Event of the temple - day and date?
Monthly Events
Monthly events at the temple include Pradosham, which occurs twice a month on the 13th day of each lunar fortnight. On these days, special rituals and poojas are performed in the evening to honor Lord Shiva. Additionally, Amavasya (new moon) and Pournami (full moon) days are marked by special poojas and celebrations.
