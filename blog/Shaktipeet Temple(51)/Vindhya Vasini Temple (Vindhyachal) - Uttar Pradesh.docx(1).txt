The temple remains open for extended hours during this period to accommodate the influx of devotees who come to seek blessings from Maa Vindhyavasi. This festival occurs twice a year, during the lunar months of Chaitra (spring) and Ashwin (autumn), which generally fall around March-April and September-October, respectively.
