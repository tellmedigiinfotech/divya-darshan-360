Annual Event of Devi Patan Mandir
The major annual event at Devi Patan Mandir is Navratri, a festival celebrated with great enthusiasm and devotion. During Navratri, a large fair is held at the temple, attracting thousands of devotees. Another significant event is Chaitra Panchami, during which the deity of Pir Ratan Nath is brought from Dang in Nepal to be worshipped along with Goddess Pateshwari. This event highlights the temple's unique cultural connection with Nepal.
