A. Main Deity:
* Maa Rajrajeshwari (Tripura Sundari): The temple is dedicated to Maa Rajrajeshwari, an incarnation of Tripura Sundari, who is one of the ten Mahavidyas in Hindu tradition. She is revered as the divine mother and the goddess of beauty, power, and compassion.
B. Other Deities (Sub Deities):
* Maa Dhumavati: Associated with the concept of widowhood and inauspiciousness, she represents the fearsome aspect of the divine feminine.
* Maa Baglamukhi: Known for her power to paralyze enemies and protect devotees, she is often invoked for success in legal and competitive battles.
* Maa Matangi: Associated with inner knowledge, arts, and wisdom, she represents the power of transformation and eloquence.
* Maa Kamala: The goddess of wealth, prosperity, and fertility, she is worshipped for blessings of material and spiritual riches.
The temple's serene and spiritually charged environment, coupled with its rich historical and cultural significance, makes it a profound place of worship and meditation.
